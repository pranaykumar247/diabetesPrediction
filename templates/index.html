Diabetes Information

Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects how your body processes glucose, a type of sugar. There are different types of diabetes, including type 1, type 2, and gestational diabetes. It's important to monitor your health and make informed decisions to manage diabetes effectively.

Diabetes can lead to various complications, including heart disease, kidney damage, and nerve problems. Managing your blood sugar levels through a healthy lifestyle, medication, and regular check-ups can help prevent these complications.

A balanced diet, regular exercise, and maintaining a healthy weight are essential for managing diabetes. Your healthcare provider can provide personalized guidance and treatment options based on your individual needs.
